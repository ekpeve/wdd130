Braids

Braids are a popular and versatile hairstyle that involves weaving three or more strands of hair together.

They can be done in a variety of styles, including box braids, French braids, and twist braids. Braids can be done with natural hair or with hair extensions, and they can last for several weeks with proper care.

Price: Ranges from $50 - $150 depending on the style

Crochet

Crochet: Crochet braids are a type of hairstyle that involves braiding hair onto a cornrow base using a crochet needle.

This technique is a great way to add volume and length to natural hair or to protect hair that is prone to damage. Crochet braids can be styled in a variety of ways, including curly, straight, or in a textured style.

Price: Ranges from $50 - $150 depending on the style

Cornrows

Cornrows are a traditional African hairstyle that involves braiding hair into narrow, raised rows that run parallel to the scalp.

This style can be done in a variety of patterns, including straight rows, zig-zag patterns, and intricate designs. Cornrows can be worn as a protective style for natural hair or with added hair extensions for length and volume. With proper care, cornrows can last for several weeks.

Price: Ranges from $30 - $150 depending on the style
