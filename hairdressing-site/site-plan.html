Home page

Transform Your Hair from Ordinary to Hair-X-Ceptional!

Welcome to Hair-X-Ceptional! I specialize in creating beautiful hairstyles that make you look and feel your best. I am a skilled and experienced haidresser. I am dedicated to delivering high-quality services that will exceed your expectations.

Here you can sign up for hairdressing appointments, view different hair styles that I have made over the years, get information about services I offer, and get to know a little bit about me. I promise that you would have an Hair-X-Ceptional experience that would leave you feeling like a new person with a head-turning hairstyle.

About Me

My name is Rebecca Adekunle, but most of my friends call me Becky. As a child, I disovered my passion for hairdressing through playing with dolls. I have been styling hair for for 11 years now, starting when I was just 10 years old by braiding my classmates' hair. Over the years, I have broadened my hairdressing skills, and I am still improving on myself to become better each day.

Services

I understand that your hair is an important part of your overall look, which is why I offer a range of services to meet your individual needs. Whether you want something simple, or a stunning style for a special occasion, I have the expertise and the creativity to make it happen.

Our sessions together would be a welcoming and relaxing space, where you can sitback, unwind and let me take care of your hair. I only use the best products and techniques to ensure that your hair stays healthy and beautiful, no matter what style you choose.

I also provide helpful tips and advice on how to maintain and care for your hair between visits. Good information is key to achieving great results. I want you to feel confident and informed about your hair care routine.

I look forward to helping you achieve the perfect hairstyle for you. Book an appointment now! and let's get started.
